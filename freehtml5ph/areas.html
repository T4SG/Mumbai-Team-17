Areas Affected



 
       Eight million people have been affected by the massive earthquake in Nepal - more than a quarter of the 
		 country's population - the United Nations says.Internatonal aid has started arriving but there is still huge need - 1.4 million require food aid, the UN said.
The 7.8-magnitude quake hit Nepal on Saturday destroying buildings in Kathmandu and severely affecting rural areas across the region.
The death toll has risen to 4,310, with almost 8,000 injured, officials say.
