A student from 16 De Septiembre School in Veracruz, Mexico shared his story:
I am 11 years old and I studied at a very poor school with very small rooms. Now, thanks to Happy Hearts I have a large school in which we are all delighted. The school is very nice and everyone can study at ease. When I grow up, I want to be a doctor because I like to help others. Now, in my new school there have been many changes, there are no accidents and classrooms are more spacious and we are more healthy.

Days Are Happier When We Give People A Bit Of Our Heart Rather Than Piece Of Mind!!!
